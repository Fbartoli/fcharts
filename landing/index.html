Why now

Accessible finance stopped being optional.

The EU Accessibility Act took effect in June 2025 and names banking and financial services explicitly. ADA web lawsuits keep climbing, and procurement increasingly demands a VPAT. An inaccessible price chart is now a compliance liability — but ripping out your fast canvas charts for slow SVG isn't an option either. Sightline is the way out of that bind.
