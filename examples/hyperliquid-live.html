fcharts — Hyperliquid live candles

Every trade on a Hyperliquid perp market streams in over WebSocket and is aggregated into OHLC candles client-side — the forming candle updates in place via amendLast(), completed ones land with append(), and traded volume fills the panel below. Your browser connects straight to api.hyperliquid.xyz, no backend. Raw ticks are kept, so switching the candle interval re-aggregates the history you've collected.

Try BTC, ETH, SOL — any Hyperliquid perp coin.
